The Russian End of Game Statement

I don't have a lot to say about this game. It was my first Dip game, and I did not play it very well. But I've learned a lot from it.

I've made some serious mistakes, and after losing St. Pete and Moscow all I could do was to be a buffer between Turkey and Germany, and make German life as difficult as I could, to help Turkey. I could not do much damage, of course.

Congratulations on Turkey and thank you for the game.
