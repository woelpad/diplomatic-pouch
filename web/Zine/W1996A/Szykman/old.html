What Happened in The Pouch in 1996

This is just a sampling of some of the things that were done at The Pouch since last year's major expansion:

  • The Online Resources section has grown from a single page to several sections containing links to over 65 resources in all.
  • Created the maps page, our most popular resource, which currently has links to over 100 Diplomacy maps in a variety of formats for the standard game and popular variants.
  • Created the Hobby Registry, which now contains over 250 people.
  • Maintained a convention calendar and information about clubs and organizations in the Face-to-face section.
  • Expanded the Email and Postal sections.
  • Accumulated a hefty number of accolades and reviews (if you haven't already, see the amusing review excerpts listed at the top of the "And What They're Saying" part of the accolades page).
  • And of course, we've brought you another year's worth of the finest zine around.
