What's Brand New in The Pouch

Here's a list of the brand new things you'll find in our pages (as of the release of the W1996A issue of the zine) as part of our New Year's remodeling:

  • We've moved! David Kovar has kindly made disk space at his site available, and we are greatly indebted to him for it. Our new URL is http://www.diplom.org/DipPouch (We'll be keeping the front page at both sites so that links to the front page do not need to be updated, but update links you have to any other pages). Thanks to this move, we now have cgi capabililty again. Because of this, The Pouch has some new functionality already, with more to come in the future.
  • We've added a variants page to the Online Resources section as a complement to our maps page.
  • You can now search for information in the rec.games.diplomacy newsgroup archives.
  • Frustrated at not being able to get into a popular type of game on the Internet judges? Check out our new game queues and sign up there.
  • You can now jump to our most-often-accessed resource, the maps page, directly from the front page.
  • We've created a judge registration page so that you can register with any of the PBEM Internet judges directly from the web.
  • The hobby registry has been updated. Now that we have CGI capability again, it is once again searchable and now you can add new entries directly, using an online form.
  • Ted Miller has come on board as the maintainer of the E-mail section. Look for that section to get the attention that we didn't have time to provide.
