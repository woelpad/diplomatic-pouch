THE VIDEO ACADEMY

A/I & Tempo

by Chris Martin

Chris Martin, who is the WDC 1998 champion among many other titles and who you may remember as the man who singlehandedly video-recapped the WDC 2016 event, has placed himself once more in front of the camera for what promises to be a high-caliber series of teachings on Diplomacy.

Austria/Italy is an alliance perfectly well suited to demonstrate the importance and value of tempo in a Diplomacy game.
