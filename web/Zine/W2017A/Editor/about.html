ABOUT THE DIPLOMATIC POUCH

by Mario Huys


Bells are ringing in the New Year

For the second year in a row we lost the server where the Pouch was stored on, yet there we are again. We're like that edelweiss flower high up in the Alps that cracks under the weight of an avalanche, but seeds itself to sprout back a few months later. As it's the season, let's celebrate this with a Christmas and New Year special. Just songs and games and just a tiny bit of review — well, quite a bit actually.

For those who wonder what happened with the regular edition, particularly the Fall 2017 Movement issue that was still in preparation at the time of the server loss (after the untimely death of its owner, Alain Tésio), rest assured that it will be produced. The elves are working on it.

Enjoy the Season!
