Mail Received Concerning
The 2016 Lexicon Diplomacy Tournament

From Nate Cockerill (nate.cockerill@facebook.com):

Here are the details of Lexicon.

Lexicon is a three round Diplomacy tournament using the Carnage scoring system. The Diplomacy tournament will be held at the Lexicon Tabletop Gaming Convention in Lexington Kentucky at the a Clarion Convention Center at 1950 Newton Pike.

April 23rd
  Round 1 9:45 am Board Call 10:00 am start.
  Round 2 5:45 pm Board Call 6:00 pm start.
April 24th
  Round 3 9:45 am Board Call 10:00 start.

Round 3 will be followed by a short award ceremony with plaques for the top 7 countries as well as the overall winner. Lexicon will be a part of the 2016 Central Shuffle. For more info on the con please visit http://lexicongaming.com/2016/.

Thanks,
Nate

Mail Received Concerning
DO REAL DIPPERS READ REAL BOOKS ABOUT REAL DIP ANYMORE?

From Larry Peery (peery@ix.netcom.com):

We're looking for contributors for a new TDP feature. We need people who write books, read books, and like to write about books. The books can be about Diplomacy (the game) or diplomacy-related books that might be of interest to Dippers. Solo efforts, by writers or reviewers, are welcome and group efforts (e.g. multiple reviews of the same book) are equally welcome.

If you were (or are) a fan of Brian Lamb's long-running C-SPAN series, BOOKNOTES, then you should be writing for and reading this new and, hopefully, regular TDP series. If you've got a book you've written and want reviewed or a book you've read and want to review, drop me a line. The worst thing I can say, "I know you can do better. Write, or read, it again!" :-)

Larry
