DING DONG MARIO'S ON HIGH

by Larry Peery

To the tune of "Ding Dong Merrily On High"
Lyrics by Larry Peery
With ye mighty apologies to George Ratcliffe Woodward


Ding Dong Mario's On High
in Dip'dom the dots are falling.
Ding dong verify the center count
Is riv'n with Dippers' singing.
Gloria, Hosanna in excelsis!

E'ven so, here below, below
Let stil-let-toes be swinging
And "lo, lo, lo"
By player and GM be singing
Gloria, Hosanna, in excelsis!

Pray you dutifully stab
Your matin blahs ye Dippers
May you beautifully time
Your evetime stab, ye Dippers
Gloria, Hosanna in excelsis!



Larry Peery
