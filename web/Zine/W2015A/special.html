Christmas 2015 Special Issue



The Editor: About The Diplomatic Pouch Christmas Special
Eighteen years later we have a new round of Christmas carol spoofs. Enjoy!
The Editorial Board: Yes, Virginia!
Merry Christmas and Ho-ho-ho! The Pouch has his own Santa and his name is Larry Peery. But even we sometimes doubt that he is real.
Kevin Burt: The Frog and the Scorpion
An illustrious fable gets a Diplomatic twist.
Kevin Burt: Let's go!
No better way to start the carousel.
Kevin Burt: Blue Christmas
A poignant cry out to Machiavelli players. If Elvis were still alive, he would surely join the fun. Wait, what, he is?
Mario Huys: Stab
Not a Christmas carol per se (not that Disney executives would mind if people thought otherwise), but the coming out of the Ice Queen conjures up the same forces solicited by us Diplomats.
Manus Hand: Silent Friend
A contrapoint from the master himself. Where there are winners, there are bound to be losers.
Kevin Burt: I'll be in Rome by Christmas
Kevin has made himself a promise. And no green fleets will keep him from it.
The Editors: The Year in Trivia
All the small things you learned this year from the Pouch in 10 simple questions — or is that 11?
Larry Peery: Ding Dong Mario's on High *Bonus Track*
Angels are singin' the Dippers' mantra. Look out, they're fallin' from the sky!
