Introduction

This time the Academy takes a look at the end of game statements for for the game HGWells. This was the first completed game under the "It Came From Outer Space" variant rules. Dan Percival, who moderated the game HGWells, created the ICFOS variant and I helped him sharpen it. If you are unfamiliar with the rules of this variant, feel free to read them before looking at the EOG statements. Otherwise, on with the show.
