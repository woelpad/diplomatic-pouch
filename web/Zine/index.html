The Zine

The Diplomatic Pouch 'zine, the on-line magazine for the players of Hasbro's game of Diplomacy and its many variants, is published five times a year, beginning with a Spring Movement issue, followed in turn by a Spring Retreat issue, a Fall Movement issue, a Fall Retreat issue, and a Winter Adjustment issue. From this page, you can quickly jump to any of the issues of the magazine.

All the information you need on the publication schedule and how you can contribute to The Zine can be found on the "About The Zine" page. Give it a look.
