ABOUT THE POUCH

by the Editor

Welcome to the Fall 2013 Movement issue of the Pouch!

It's that time of year again! The WDC in Paris is over. Students are returning to school — and, I hope, discovering the wonderful world of online Diplomacy if they haven't already.

On our end, we seem to be chugging along! I think we have a good selection of articles in this issue, and I hope you'll agree.

However, we can always use more! Please, submit your own article to editor@diplom.org for the next issue, the Fall 2013 Retreat issue at the end of October, just in time for Hallowe'en. Remember, the continuing health of the Pouch Zine depends on us receiving good articles. So please send us something! We cover all sorts of topics related to Diplomacy — so please, pick an aspect of the game that interests you, and write something about it!

Enjoy the Pouch!



Charles Roburn
The (Managing) Editor
(editor@diplom.org)
