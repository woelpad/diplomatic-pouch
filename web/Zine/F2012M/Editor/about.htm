ABOUT THE POUCH

by the Editor

Welcome to the Fall 2012 Movement issue of the Pouch!

As promised, we've put together another issue. Many thanks to Chris Babcock, who solicited the articles for it, and to the contributors who took the time to write for us.

While this issue is modest in size, I hope the fact that it's here will inspire more hobbyists to step forward and participate more actively in the next one. As I've said many time before — the Pouch Zine needs YOU. (Yes, that means YOU, sir or madam! I'm talking to you!) We need to keep the momentum going, and build on it to bring this publication back up to its former level of production and excellence. So do your part — volunteer today!

Enjoy the Pouch!



Charles Roburn
The Editor (Retired)
(editor@diplom.org)
