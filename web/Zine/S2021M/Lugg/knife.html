The Game of Knife

by Oliver Lugg

This hilarious video teaches the principles of playing Diplomacy against the backdrop of the final game of the 2012 World Diplomacy Team Tournament, then goes on to explain paradoxes, game theory and much more in a tongue-in-cheek, self-deprecating tone. You will unfortunately need to click the YouTube link to watch, because policy.
