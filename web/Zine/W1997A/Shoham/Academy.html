The Diplomacy Academy

Introduction

For this edition of the Diplomacy Academy, I have chosen the game troi, in which I played Turkey. This was a crowded (11 players -- with no neutral supply centers) No-Press variant game. Winning this game required going through the various phases of a normal game and adapting the strategies to the NoPress nature of the game at each stage.
