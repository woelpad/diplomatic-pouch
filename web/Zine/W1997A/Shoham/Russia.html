Russia's End Of Game Statement from "troi"

At the beginning I tried to expand in Scandinavia and control the south, waiting to see what would have happened among Austria, Balkans and Turkey. I do succeded in getting Sweden from Norway, and to mantain the rest of my Homeland.

Then two facts surprised me:

  • the continuously shifting support given by the Balkans to either me or Turkey that led to the loss of Sevastopol.
  • being a novice, I just did not realize that Sweden and Norway are neighbouring countries ALSO for fleets; so I let England into Norway when I could have easily continued to buonce there, and, later I failed to support my fleet in Sweden with the A STP.
So I was reduced to four SCs, while Turkey was growing in the south, and Sevastopol was no longer defensible, and England became stronger in the north. So I tried my chances against Austria, to earn the SCs that would have saved me, but even if I was successful there, I could do nothing against the combined Turko-English attack against my homeland. So at the end, having seen Turkey growing I decided to do my best to slow him. And then I started having net access problem, so I was forced to resign.

As a first experience, I have to say that I enjoyed quite a lot this game, I learned a lot from the tactical point of view, with some (costly) mistakes. Being able to concentrate only on the board, and not on the negotiations, I could devote my (scarce) time to understanding the likely scenarios and to weight the decisions.

I wish that more games of the Nopress kind would be available.

Leonello
