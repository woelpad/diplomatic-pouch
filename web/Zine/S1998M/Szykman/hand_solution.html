DipSticks

Manus Hand

Here is another puzzle for you. This one is an "acrostick" (my own personal favorite in the family of pencil puzzles). To complete this puzzle, simply print this page then fit the names of the thirty-four supply centers into the grid. That may not be as easy as it sounds, though! There is only one possible solution, but how will you know which of the many six-letter supply centers goes into each of the spots for a six-letter word? Only by a thoroughly enjoyable application of a little grey matter.

Have fun! E-mail a copy of your solution as soon as you have it, and you'll see your name in electrons in the next issue of the Zine!
