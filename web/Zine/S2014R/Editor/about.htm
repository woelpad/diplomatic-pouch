ABOUT THE POUCH

by the Editor

Welcome to the Spring 2014 Retreat issue of the Pouch!

I'm proud to point out that this issue marks a milestone in the publishing of the Pouch Zine: this is our tenth consecutive issue since the Fall 2012 Movement issue. This is the first time since the Pouch began publication in 1995 that we've managed to have ten issues published on schedule, in a row, without interruption!

I am very grateful to Chris Babcock for his tireless efforts to get material for us to publish — without his help, we couldn't have done it! I am also grateful to our many regular contributors — Mario Huys, Sergio Lidsell, Alex Hartl, Doug Kent and Jim Burgess at Diplomacy World, and of course Larry Peery — and the many others who have kept us going for two steady years.

However… we always need more!

Please, join the ranks of these illustrious contributors, and send your articles to editor@diplom.org for the Fall 2014 Movement issue at the end of August. We look forward to hearing from you!

Enjoy the Pouch!



Charles Roburn
The (Managing) Editor
(editor@diplom.org)
