WDC 2007 in Vancouver

There is always help needed for hosting a WDC! The upcoming WDC 2007 in Vancouver could use help in filling the following positions:

  • French-Canadian go getter to work the Eastern Provinces for Canadians to get to Vancouver.
  • European Country coordinators: it would be great if we could get French, German, Dutch, British, Irish, Italian, and Scandinavian representatives to help dig up support in their local areas.
  • Down Under Cheerleaders: Sean Coleman and family are coming, and anyone else to help with the Rah Rah down there would be good. (Normally I might say 'Rooting' For WDC, but that has a rather obscene connotation Down Under...)
