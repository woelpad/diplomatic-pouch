2002 HOBBY AWARDS BALLOT

Jim Burgess, Chair of the Hobby Awards Committee: The Hobby Awards have a long and proud tradition in the Diplomacy Hobby and I am continuing an effort to expand the scope and reach of the awards. This year we again have eight nominees in each category. Please publicize this ballot, reproduce and send it to your Diplomatic mailing lists. Please consider the whole ballot carefully, use the web pages for research, and vote in one, two, three or all four award categories.

The 2002 Hobby Awards generally are for hobby activities during the calendar year 2001. Congratulate ALL of the nominees for their service and achievements and please vote!
