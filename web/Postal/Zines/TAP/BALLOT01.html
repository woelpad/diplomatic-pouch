2001 HOBBY AWARDS BALLOT

Jim Burgess, New Chair of the Hobby Awards Committee: I am very honored to be taking over the chairmanship of this committee this year. The Hobby Awards have a long and proud tradition in the US Diplomacy Hobby and I am continuing an effort to expand the scope and reach of the awards. This year we have eight nominees in each category, except for the Rod Walker Award for Literary Achievement where the Founder of the Feast weighs in, so we have nine nominees in that category. Please publicize this ballot, reproduce and send it to your Diplomatic mailing lists.
