Terrain Build Costs
Plains, Towns: 1 (basic build cost)
Mountain: +2 per half-hex
River: +2 per hex-side

Actual Build Costs
1: Plains - Plains
3: Plains - River - Plains; Plains - Mountain
5: Mountain - Mountain; Plains - River - Mountain

Terrain Notes
The river Dnieper costs 5 to cross for all river hexes
between Kherson and F55/F56.
Lakes (e.g. E12 and L24/K25) are impassable as are
foreign hexes that are not marked as connection points.

Start Positions
3 to 5 players.
All start at Kyyiv. On the first turn each player may only exit Kyyiv through one hex.

Specials
1. Russia: reach A74, G68 or N65
2. Belarus: reach N58 or N45
3. Poland: reach H43 or K44
4. Slovakia or Hungary: Reach C42 or N1
5. Romania or Moldova: Reach M4, D12 or J14
6. Any coastal town
