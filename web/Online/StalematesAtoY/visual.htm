Visual Index to Stalemate Positions

Matthew Self

This index should help you quickly find those stalemate positions that could be relevant to your particular situation. All of the lines are arranged in a grid according to the region they control. Those holding England are along the top and left; those holding Turkey are along the bottom and right. In the center you'll find the peculiar position that holds England and Turkey, but nothing in between. Click on each maps to see the variations and extensions of each of these nine positions.
