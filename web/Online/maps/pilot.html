Pilot Diplomacy Map Information

Have you ever been stuck somewhere and wished you had a Diplomacy map at hand? Grab one of these maps for Palm PDAs.

The map is in Imageviewer format. If you haven't shelled out the money for Imageviewer, you can also view it with other less functional but capable freeware Imageviewer viewers (such as TinyViewer).

To view the map, download and expand the diplomacy.pdb file, and hotsync it to your PDA. The viewer should recognize the file.

Download map 1 stuffed & binhexed or zipped or try map 2 zipped.
