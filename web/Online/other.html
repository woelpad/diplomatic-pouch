Quick Reference Page
(All resources on one page)
The Diplomacy Ring
A Webring for players of the Game of Diplomacy.

Diplomacy on ICQ
This site coordinates the playing of Diplomacy (real-time and PBEM-style) on ICQ, a new-generation Internet chat system.

Sean Lorber's Diplomacy AI
An ongoing effort to develop a Diplomacy AI program. This page has a link to the latest version of the software.

The Emissary
Another resource for Diplomacy players with a particularly useful tabular presentation of links to other sites.

ftp.ugcs.caltech.edu FTP Archive (mirrored at www.diplom.org)
Currently the main archive of Diplomacy information having a variety of useful items, including maps for judge-supported variants in several different formats, judge source code, HTML versions of judge information files, back issues of several Diplomacy zines and various other information about the game.

ftp.sentex.ca FTP Archive
An archive of Nick Fitzpatrick's stuff. Includes postscript maps for several different variants as well as his Hall of Fame, Boardman and Miller number logs and judge Statistics files.

Internet Boardman Number and Miller Number game logs.
These are lists of every Boardman and Miller games played on any Judge, listed by number. All Diplomacy games are eligible for numbers, Boardman for standard games and Miller for non-standard variant games. Nick Fitzpatrick is the current Internet Boardman and Miller Number Custodian. More information about Boardman and Miller numbers can be found on the Boardman Number Home Page and the Miller Number Home Page.
