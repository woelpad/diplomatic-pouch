Quick Reference Page
(All resources on one page)

These pages contain rules, maps, articles and general web pages for over 170 Diplomacy variants.

Links to the maps are now available on the variants pages. Maps that are listed in bold are maps that can be viewed directly through a graphical browser. With the exception of browser-viewable maps and items marked with asterisks, all maps are unix-compressed and need to be uncompressed to view or print.

Also, don't forget to check out Variants A to Z, a library containing descriptions (but usually not detailed rules) for tons of Diplomacy variants.

And for an even bigger list, check out variantbank.org which has a list of all known variants (with the rules to approx. 400 of them).

Please send additions or updates to the contact at the bottom of the page.
