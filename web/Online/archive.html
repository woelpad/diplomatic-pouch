FAQ
The rec.games.diplomacy FAQ section contains answers to questions on Diplomacy in general, and relates to Face-To-Face, Play-By-Mail and Play-By-E-Mail play.

The Judge FAQ
Contains answers to questions specifically relating to the use of the Diplomacy Judge software written by Ken Lowe. Last updated 28 Dec 1998, so your help would be appreciated in drafting an updated version. Please contact us at the address below.

Strategy and Tactics Articles
Links to well over 200 articles that will help you improve your Diplomacy game. The list includes articles on how to play each power, opening strategies, the endgame, and much more, as well as a list of articles on how to play various Diplomacy variants.
