Diplomacy Buyer's Guide

Last updated 24 August, 2000
Please click here to submit any new information or to update information that is out of date.

If you are in the market for the game of Diplomacy, read on. Although this initially started out as a one-time article in the Zine, a couple of followup requests from people as well as continued inquiries on rec.games.diplomacy have prompted me to try to maintain this guide on an ongoing basis.

The Diplomacy Buyer's Guide is divided up into the following three sections:

  • Where to buy new editions of the Diplomacy boardgame
    Information on buying new copies of Diplomacy editions (in and out of print).
  • Where to buy new editions of Diplomacy variants
    Information on buying new copies of Diplomacy variants (in and out of print).
  • Where to buy various used editions of Diplomacy and its variants
    Discusses buying used editions of Diplomacy on online auctions.
  • Where to buy the MicroProse computer version of Diplomacy
    Information on finding good deals on the MicroProse computer version of Diplomacy (and software in general) in the U.S., as well as information about where to buy the MicroProse computer version of Diplomacy outside the U.S. which some people have had trouble finding.

Hope you find this information useful. Happy shopping!
