Face to Face Diplomacy in Canada!


The 2002 Canadian Diplomacy Champion is Steve Mills of Alberta!


The popularity of Face to Face Diplomacy haw waxed and waned in Canada over the years. Currently the only annual tournaments are in Toronto and in Calgary, but there appears to be a renewed interest in Victoria, Vancouver, and Montreal.
