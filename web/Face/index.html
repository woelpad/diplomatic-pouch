Informal Gatherings

Looking for a place to sheath your knife? The Diplomatic Pouch currently lists over 200 hobbyists looking for informal face-to-face gatherings. To look for games near you, just select the country in which you're located. If you're already in here, don't forget to register yourself in the Hobby Registry!

Please notify us of any other gatherings or solicitations for the play of Diplomacy, and we will add them to the list.

Clubs & Organizations

Clubs and groups dedicated to Diplomacy--all looking for another ally or a dedicated enemy. The Diplomatic Pouch currently lists over three dozen Diplomacy clubs and organizations. To look for clubs near you, just select the country in which you're located.

Please notify us of any other Diplomacy Clubs or Organizations, and we will add them to the list.
