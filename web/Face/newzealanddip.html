Face to Face Diplomacy in New Zealand!


The current New Zealand Diplomacy Champion is Grant Torrie of Auckland!


Diplomacy is alive and well in New Zealand. There are a several annual tournaments, and dozens of acitve players. If you're looking for information on Diplomacy in New Zealand, a good place to start is at the web site for the Diplomacy Association of Australia and New Zealand.

2002 tournaments in New Zealand include:
January 23-24: Waikato Diplomacy Open (Hamilton)
Mid July: Auckland Diplomacy Championships (Auckland)
Late August: Wellington Diplomacy Championship (Wellington)
Late January: New Zealand Diplomacy Championship (Auckland)

In the mean time, check out results of New Zealand Diplomacy tournaments:
New Zealand Diplomacy Championships: 1998, 1999, 2000, 2001.
Waikato Diplomacy Open: 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002.
Wellington Diplomacy Open: 2000, 2001.
Auckland Championsip: 2001.
