Tournament List

The ratings currently include events through October 16, 2004. This is a list of all events from which ratings are currently being calculated. Currently, the DPTR is calculated based on the results from 378 events. Most of these events have taken place since January 1, 1995; plus it includes the results of all the World Championships in 1988, 1990, 1992, and 1994; and it includes the results of the European Championships in 1993 and 1994. An additional 68 events are recorded in the database, but do not have any impact on player ratings. Additional ratings modifications have been made to select players on the basis of older events which they won, but for which complete results are not available.
