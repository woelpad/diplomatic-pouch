Welcome!

Glad you could make it to the Vancouver Diplomacy site. This site as evolved from it's original purpose of keeping players across the Northwest informed about the annual Victoria tournament into a hub for all Canadian players to keep up on Diplomacy hobby happenings. The Victoria Tournament moved to White Rock, just outside of Vancouver in an effort to become better prepared for a World Diplomacy Championship (WDC) bid in 2005.

We're happy to report that in conjunction with Portland and Seattle, through the Northwest Diplomacy Group, our bid for WDC 2007 in Vancouver, BC was awarded in July 2005.
