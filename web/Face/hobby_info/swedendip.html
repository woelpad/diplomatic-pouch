Face to Face Diplomacy in Sweden!


The 2001 Swedish Diplomacy Champion was Chetan Radia, of the United Kingdom!


The Swedish Diplomacy hobby came together in the late 1980s, and has been going strong ever since. As a regular destination for foreign players looking to expand their horizons, it hosted the European Championships in 1994, and the World Championships in 1997. Even a brief look at the Diplomatic Pouch Tournament Ratings will show you that the Swedish players are a force to be reconed with! For more information about Diplomacy in Sweden, check out the Swedish Diplomacy Association website.
