The results below use the "American Diplomacy Rating" system. The format is as follows:

The first letter is for the result of the game: W,D,L for win, draw, or elimination. The second number is the board number, and the final letter is the Power played.

The scoring for the "American Diplomacy Ratings" system awards 61 points to a solo win. No points are awarded based on the number of supply centers captured, and instead rely on the number of Powers participating in the draw. Point awards for draws are listed below:

2-way=30pts3-way=20pts4-way=15pts
5-way=12pts6-way=10pts7-way=8pts
