Face to Face Diplomacy in Australia!


The current Australian Diplomacy Champion is Tristan Lee of Victoria!


Diplomacy has a long history in Australia. After lots of activity in the early 1990's, Australian Diplomacy faded from the international scene by the middle of the decade. But over the last few years, the Australian hobby has stormed back with a dramatic increase in local hobby involvement. The Australians have recently been rewarded for their hard work with Canberra's selection as the site for the 2002 World Diplomacy Championships! If you're looking for additional information on Diplomacy in Australia, a good place to start is at the web site for the Diplomacy Association of Australia and New Zealand.
