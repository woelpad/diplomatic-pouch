Face to Face Diplomacy in France!



There has always been lots of Diplomacy going on in France, though often players outside of the country didn't know about it. Fortunately, there has been a renewed contact between the French hobby and the rest of the world. If you're looking for information on Diplomacy in France, a good place to start is at the web site for the French Diplomacy Association.

Results of French Diplomacy tournaments:
World Diplomacy Championship: 1995, 2001.
European Diplomacy Championship: 1993, 2000.
Ile de France Championship 1996-1997: First Leg, Second Leg, Third Leg, Fourth Leg, Final Leg.
French Championship: 2/1997, 10/1997.
French Cup: 1996.
Boulogne Billancourt: 2001.
Brittany Cup: 1997.
Eragny Open: 1996.
Evry Open: 1996.
Olstelen Trophy: 2001.
Paris Open: 1/1995, 9/1995, 11/1995, 1996, 1997.
Vinvennes Open: 1998.
