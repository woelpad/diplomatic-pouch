ASCII Version of the Standard Map

You can receive a copy of this file via E-mail by sending your local Judge a message with "get map.top" in the body. 

Regions with "."s are water.
Regions with "="s around their names are supply centres.
Regions are adjacent if and only if they share a "|" or a "-"
   on their boundaries.

Map based on that in House of Games Corporation's Diplomacy* game;
  copyright 1959 by Allan B. Calhamer.
Base map in House of Games Corp.'s game copyright by C.S.Hammond & Co., NY.
This version done originally by Jamie Andrews, jha@lfcs.ed.ac.uk, May 1989.
