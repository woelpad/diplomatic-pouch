The DP Council

The Diplomatic Pouch has undergone some major changes over its history. Originally developed as a Web magazine published five times a year and maintained by one person alone, by its fifth issue it had grown into something quite different: The Pouch is now the web site for and about Diplomacy. As The Pouch has grown, so has the number of people needed to maintain the site.

We are the DP Council, the caretakers of The Diplomatic Pouch:
