Like The Pouch...?

If you like The Diplomatic Pouch and aren't ashamed to admit it, we could use your help. There are a few places we've come across that request web page or zine reviews. However, we feel that we here aren't exactly impartial and that submitting a review ourselves would be like stuffing a ballot box. If you are interested in reviewing the Pouch, here are a few people who would be interested in hearing your opinion:
  • The Point, who identify the top 5% of web sites. If you think we are up there, here's their review page.
  • c|net, the computer network, reviews the best of the web. Their review page is located here.
  • Yahoo! Internet Life, reviews the "best sites for fun [that's us], profit and personal growth". Here's their review page.
  • FutureNet, an E-Zine with web site reviews and a section on games. You have to register to get in, but they promise that information is kept confidential and won't lead to junk e-mail. On the plus side, they say that they won't use your writing without contacting you to arrange a fee. See the suggestion box at the site after registering.
  • E-Zine Reviews accepts reviews of any E-zine. Just follow the submission guidelines link on their front page.
If you do submit a review to one or more of the sites listed above (or to any other site), please accept our thanks in advance and use the short form below to let us know which one(s). This feedback to us is anonymous; we just want to keep an eye out for reviews and remove sites from the list above once reviews are published.
